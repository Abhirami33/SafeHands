Help Rebuild Homes

The devastating floods in Assam have displaced thousands of families, leaving them without shelter or basic living conditions. Many homes were completely destroyed, forcing families to live in makeshift tents under unsafe conditions.
This campaign is focused on providing construction materials, financial aid, and labor support to rebuild safe and sustainable homes.
With each donation, we move closer to restoring stability and security for these affected communities.
Your support can give families a new beginning and a roof over their heads. Together, we can rebuild lives! ❤️

Emergency Food Drive

Thousands of families affected by the floods are in urgent need of food supplies. This campaign ensures they receive essential nutrition during this crisis.

Location: Kerala, India

Date: February 1 - April 15, 2023

Funds Raised: ₹2,76,000 / ₹4,50,000

Emergency Food Drive

The devastating floods in Kerala have left thousands of families struggling for food and essential supplies. Many have lost their homes and access to proper nutrition, putting children, elderly, and vulnerable groups at severe risk of malnutrition.
Through this campaign, we aim to distribute emergency food kits containing rice, pulses, cooking oil, dry fruits, and ready-to-eat meals.
Each kit provides sustenance for a family of four for a week, ensuring they have access to nutritious food during these tough times.
Your support can help us reach more families and ensure no one goes hungry. Join us in making a difference today! ❤️

Health Kits for Families

Following the devastating floods in Bihar, many families are facing severe health risks due to water contamination and lack of medical supplies.

Location: Bihar, India

Date: March 1 - May 1, 2023

Funds Raised: ₹1,72,000 / ₹3,30,000

Health Kits for Families

The Bihar floods of 2020 left over 83 lakh people affected, with many struggling for access to clean water and medical care. Contaminated water sources led to outbreaks of diseases such as cholera, dengue, and typhoid. This campaign focuses on distributing health kits containing essential medicines, ORS packets, masks, sanitizers, and first-aid supplies. By supporting this initiative, you help prevent the spread of diseases and ensure that families receive the medical aid they desperately need. Your contribution can save lives and protect vulnerable communities from future health crises. ❤️
