Volunteer Opportunities

Rescue

Help in search and rescue operations. Skills needed: Basic First Aid, team coordination.

Medical Aid

Provide medical care to affected individuals. Skills needed: Medical training, first aid, CPR.

Food Distribution

Assist in distributing food to those in need. Skills needed: Organization, patience, and stamina.

Logistics

Support in managing supplies and equipment. Skills needed: Logistics coordination, resource management.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Register to become a volunteer by filling out the sign-up form.

2. Get Assigned

After registration, get assigned a role based on your profile.

3. Ground Support

Join your assigned team and assist in relief efforts.

4. Get Certified

Recieve volunteering certificate after completion.
